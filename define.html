WHAT IS GLOBAL WARMING?

Global warming is defined as the increase in average global temperatures. This phenomenon is related to rising greenhouse gases, and we at Earthentic want to focus on its relation with temperature, sea levels, and CO2. Our goal is to use our data exploration to provide context and understand the implications of global warming.
